The Half-Day "Colonia" Loop

Time	Activity	Highlights
--
11:33	Arrival	Step out the South Exit. The Cologne Cathedral (Dom) is immediately in front of you. 科隆大教堂
11:45	Dom Head inside (Free).Don't miss the Shrine of the Three Magi behind the altar. If you're feeling athletic, the tower climb is 533 steps for a killer view.
12:45	Lunch & Kölsch Walk 5 mins to the Old Town (Altstadt). Find a traditional Brauhaus like Früh am Dom or Peters Brauhaus. 
        Order a Kölsch (0.2L beer) and a "Halve Hahn" (rye roll with cheese).
14:15	Old Town Stroll	Walk through Alter Markt and Heumarkt. Look for the colorful houses at Fischmarkt and the Great St. 
        Martin Church for the classic "Cologne postcard" photo.
15:00	Riverside Walk	Walk along the Rhine Promenade towards the Hohenzollern Bridge (the one covered in thousands of "Love Locks").
15:30	Love Lock Bridge Walk across the bridge to the Deutz side. The view of the skyline and Cathedral from this side is the best in the city.
16:00	Quick Choice	
Option A: Visit the Lindt Chocolate Museum (further south along the river). 

Option B: Visit Museum Ludwig (next to the Dom) for Picasso and Pop Art.
17:00	Return to Hbf	Swing by the station. If you have 10 mins, grab a Brezel or a pastry from one of the many bakeries inside the station.
17:18	Departure Your train leaves.
