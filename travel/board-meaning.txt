Abfahrt = Departure
Zeit = Time
Scheduled departure time 10:17
Über (Via) = Stops along the way
Koblenz – Köln – Dortmund – Bremen

Ziel = Final destination
Gleis = Platform
Platform changes are common — always check again before boarding.

Status / Message Area
Common messages you may see:
Verspätung ca. 5 Min.
→ Delay approx. 5 minutes

Gleisänderung → Platform change
Zug fährt von Gleis X → Train departs from platform X

+5
→ 5 minutes late

Practical Reading Example
If you see:
10:20 | ICE 772 | via Hannover | Hamburg-Altona | Gleis 8 | +10
It means:
Leaves 10:20
ICE train number 772
Final destination Hamburg-Altona
Platform 8
10 minutes late
