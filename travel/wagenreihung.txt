Platform Sections (A–F)
Platforms are divided into letter zones: A — B — C — D — E — F
Each section has a blue sign hanging from the ceiling.
If your ticket says: Coach 12
And Wagenreihung shows Coach 12 stops at section D → You stand at Platform X, Section D

Each box = one coach.
1st class is usually at one end.
Bordrestaurant is usually near middle.
Coach numbers are printed on your ticket.

Train Direction
You must check the arrow: ← Hamburg → Frankfurt
If the train is reversed from normal configuration, the order flips.
Always confirm direction on the display.

Step-by-Step Strategy for 8 Adults
Before Arrival
Everyone knows their coach number.
Split into groups of 2–3 per coach.
At Station (20 min early)
Check departure board → confirm platform.
Go to platform.

Find Wagenreihung display.
Locate your coach number.
Stand at correct letter section.

When train arrives:
Door opens directly in front of you.
No running.
No crowd crossing.

Important Reality
ICE stops only 2–3 minutes.
If you stand wrong section, you will need to walk fast.
With luggage, wrong positioning is painful.
