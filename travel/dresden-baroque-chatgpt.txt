Dresden – Baroque Focus Walking Plan
Arrival: 10:28 Dresden Hbf
Departure: 18:53 Dresden Hbf
Theme: High Baroque Royal Dresden
Route: Compact Altstadt loop (minimal detours)
Total walking: ~5–6 km

This version removes secondary stops and concentrates on the most famous Baroque landmarks only.
10:35 – 11:00 Walk Dresden Hbf → Altstadt (Prager Straße → Wilsdruffer Straße)
11:00 – 12:00 Zwinger The definitive Baroque complex in Dresden.
 Courtyard visit + exterior photography.
 Optional: 30 min Old Masters Gallery (Raphael’s Sistine Madonna). This is your architectural highlight.
12:05 – 12:25 Semperoper (Exterior) Technically Neo-Renaissance, but essential in the Baroque ensemble setting.
12:25 – 12:50 Katholische Hofkirche Late Baroque court church. Interior visit (~20 min).
12:50 – 13:10 Fürstenzug Largest porcelain artwork in the world.
13:15 – 14:00 Lunch at Neumarkt area (stay central).
14:00 – 15:00 Frauenkirche Dresden The icon of Dresden Baroque. Interior visit.
 Optional dome climb (~30 min, excellent skyline view). This defines Dresden’s silhouette.
15:05 – 15:30 Brühlsche Terrasse Baroque urban planning overlooking the Elbe.
15:40 – 16:20 Cross Augustus Bridge to Neustadt Golden Rider Equestrian statue of Augustus the Strong. Short walk along Hauptstraße.
16:30 – 17:15 Free buffer time: Coffee at Neumarkt
17:15 – 17:45 Walk back to Dresden Hbf
Arrive ~18:00 (ample safety margin)
