Week 8: Guidelines for In-Class English Reading Presentation

1 Students who present will be graded in four levels: A, B, C, and D. Absence will result in an F.
2 Students who fail to present within the scheduled time will receive a one-level grade deduction.
3 Each pair of presenting students must prepare their own script, including student ID and name. One copy must be submitted to the instructor for grading.
4 The maximum presentation time for each group is 3 minutes.
